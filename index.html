About CodeStat

We are a team of graduates from renowned universities in Ghana, with diverse academic backgrounds in Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics, and Bioinformatics. Our goal is to leverage our interdisciplinary expertise to provide innovative solutions in data analytics, research support, and software development, helping organizations harness the power of data to achieve meaningful impact.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, CodeStat was established by a group of passionate graduates who recognized the untapped potential of data-driven decision-making in Ghana.

Through our collective experiences, we observed a significant gap in how data is understood and utilized by many Ghanaian institutions. This realization inspired us to join forces with backgrounds in Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics, and Bioinformatics to empower organizations with the tools, knowledge, and technologies needed to harness data effectively and drive informed decisions for growth and innovation.
